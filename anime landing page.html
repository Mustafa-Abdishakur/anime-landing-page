Rise of the shield hero

Show summary

The show revolves around one character named Iwatanai. one day when he was walking back from work or just walking around (I dont remember what he was doing deal with it), he died (I dont remember how he died). he woke up in another world where magic is a thing and and other iseki fantasy stuff that people love these days and was given the title shield hero. He is now one of the four cardinal heroes (arrow, sword and spear heros who were also summoned) who's duty is to fight the wave which is a swarm of demons or whatever those things are that are summoned at a specific time in order to save the world. the dude job gets complicated when he got framed for crimes he didnt do from the first episode and was hated by everyone. the story revolves around his struggle to do his heroic deeds which he has to do to get back home and how he overcame it with the power of friendship like any other generic show.

Characters

Shield hero iwatani

Naofumi Iwatani is a regular college student and otaku who found a book titled "Four Heavenly Weapon's Manual" which told the story of four Heroes summoned to defend another world. In an instant this became his reality as he found himself in a new world and declared one of the Legendary Heroes. Summoned as the Hero of the Shield Naofumi briefly fantasized about the adventures he would lead only to be betrayed and have his name dragged through the mud. His eyes opened and filled with deep anger, Naofumi vows to get his revenge.

Raphtalia

A sickly tanuki demi-human child enslaved by humans, Raphtalia had given up hope after the deaths of her parents and several abusive masters. Purchased by Naofumi at a discount and given a weapon she was made to act as his sword, attacking where he could not. Quickly cured of her sickness, Raphtalia became a skilled fighter and fell in love with Naofumi.

Filo

A Filorial hatched from an egg that Naofumi bought from the Slave Merchant. Due to being hatched by a Legendary Hero, Filo is a Filorial Queen and so stronger, smarter, and faster than most Filorials. She can shapeshift between her Filorial form and a human form, a pre-adolescent girl with wings.

Princess Melty Q Melromarc

The second princess of the Melromarc Kingdom. She was raised separately by her mother Mirelia, away from the influence of AultClay and her sister Malty. As such, she's much kinder and more fit to rule.

Ren Amagi

A 16-year-old high school student summoned to be the Hero of the Sword. Due to preferring solo play in MMOs he is poor at teamwork and his party suffers from poor leadership. He tends to act without thinking things through but is quick to accept and try to atone for his mistakes.

Motoyasu Kitamura

A 21-year-old University Student summoned to be the Hero of the Spear. He is extremely popular with women and views it as his duty to protect them and show off his cool side.

Itsuki Kawasumi

A 17-year-old high school student summoned to be the Hero of the Bow. He wants to appear as the "humble hero" who appears when others are in danger to protect them and then disappears, but only so he can receive praise.
